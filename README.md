# 影 Manebite

**Learn Japanese by shadowing native speakers — one sentence at a time.**

Manebite lets you practice Japanese the way language learners swear by: shadowing. You listen to a native speaker, then you repeat. No textbooks, no flashcards — just you and real Japanese audio.

---

## What it does

Paste any Japanese YouTube video URL → Manebite extracts every sentence from the subtitles → you shadow them one by one at your own pace.

---

## How to use it

**1. Add a video**
Paste a YouTube URL of any Japanese video that has subtitles. Podcasts, news segments, vlogs, dramas — anything works as long as it has Japanese captions.

**2. Go to the session**
Click **Shadow →** on any video to open the shadowing session.

**3. Shadow sentence by sentence**
Each sentence plays one at a time. Listen, then repeat out loud.

- **Space** — play / pause
- **N** — next sentence
- **P** — previous sentence
- **L** — toggle loop (repeat the same sentence until you're ready)
- **R** — toggle romaji

**4. Look up words**
Select any word in the sentence to instantly look it up — reading and meaning pop up without leaving the page.

**5. Track your progress**
Sign in to save your progress. Come back anytime and pick up exactly where you left off.
